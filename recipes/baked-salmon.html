Baked Salmon Dinner

Ingredients

  • 1 pound salmon fillet
  • 1/2 cup lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup butter, melted
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried dill
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  2. Place salmon fillet in a lightly greased 9x13 inch baking dish.
  3. In a small bowl, mix together lemon juice, butter, garlic, dill, salt, and pepper. Pour over salmon.
  4. Bake in preheated oven for 15 to 20 minutes, or until salmon flakes easily with a fork.
