Pizza

Pizza Dinner

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza crust
  • 1 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
  2. Spread pizza sauce over pizza crust.
  3. Bake in preheated oven for 10 minutes.
  4. Remove from oven, and top with mozzarella cheese.
  5. Return to oven, and bake until cheese is melted, about 5 minutes.
